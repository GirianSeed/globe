Description of B-Spline sample program

Å@

This program shows how to use VU1 to draw a B-spline curved surface where 4x4 control points are triangulated 4 times for U and V.

  • The control points for the B-spline curved surface are provided as header files (see files grid.h, torus.h).

  • Open/Closed in the U, V directions are handled.

  • Division counts in parametric directions U, V are fixed to 4 (t=0.0, 0.25, 0.5, 0.75).

  • Triangle division, calculation of the normal vector, lighting and texture ST calculations are performed in VU1 microcode.
